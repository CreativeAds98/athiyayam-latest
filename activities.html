Empowering Communities Through Action

We don't just speak about change; we create it. From classrooms to clinics, our activities are designed to uplift the underserved.

Education Support

We believe education is the most powerful weapon to change the world. Our trust identifies students from low-income families and provides them with full educational support.

  • Scholarships: Financial aid for school and college fees.
  • Back-to-School Kits: Distributing bags, uniforms, and stationery.
  • Mentorship: Career guidance sessions for high schoolers.

Medical Assistance

Good health should not be a privilege. Athiyayam Trust works tirelessly to bring healthcare to rural doorsteps and support critical medical cases.

  • Health Camps: Free general checkups, eye camps, and dental screening.
  • Emergency Aid: Financial support for surgeries and medication.
  • Rehabilitation: Supporting centers for special needs children.

Social Welfare & Environment

We are committed to the holistic development of society, which includes caring for our environment and ensuring no one goes hungry.

  • Annadhanam: Regular food distribution drives for the homeless.
  • Green Drives: Tree planting and lake cleanup initiatives.
  • Disaster Relief: Immediate mobilization of resources during crises.
