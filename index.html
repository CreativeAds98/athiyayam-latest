About Athiyayam

Athiyayam Charitable Trust works to uplift lives through compassion, education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. Our focus is on dignity, care, and long-term social impact.

Our Activities

Athiyayam Charitable Trust works across education, healthcare, empowerment, and community development to create inclusive and sustainable social impact.

Education & Child Development

  • Education for Street Children
  • Child Welfare & Protection

Healthcare & Nutrition

  • Healthcare Access & Medical Camps
  • Maternal & Adolescent Health
  • Nutrition for Women & Children
  • Rural Water, Sanitation & Hygiene (WASH)

Community & Social Welfare

  • Women Empowerment
  • Community Welfare & Social Development
  • Minority Welfare Programs
  • Senior Citizen Care

Environment & Culture

  • Environmental Care & Awareness
  • Cultural Heritage Promotion

Disaster Relief

  • Immediate relief during natural & man-made disasters
  • Distribution of food, water, clothing & essentials

Disability Support & Inclusion

  • Inclusive education & learning support
  • Assistive devices & accessibility support
  • Skill training & livelihood opportunities
  • Awareness programs for social inclusion
